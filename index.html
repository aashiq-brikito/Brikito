BRIKCHAT · IN DEVELOPMENT v1.0 · the chat with Brik AI inside

BrikChat, your site chat with Brik built in.

Brik reads every message, voice note, image and document your team posts and tags the intent: update, attendance, enquiry or ticket. Material requests become structured indents, progress feeds the dashboard, and every reply or order is drafted for your PM to approve before it sends. Anyone can also DM Brik privately and ask, in plain language, about stock, attendance, approvals or drawings.

A.Inside your project chats

Brik reads everything posted. You see the structured version.

Every voice note, image and document is captured with sender, time and type. Brik tags the intent, pulls out the figures, and turns it into something actionable, a material indent, an attendance count, a progress update, a flag, each one linked back to the original message. Replies and orders are drafted, never auto-sent.

Commitments

Four non-negotiables, written before the code.

i. Authority

Your PM is the checker. Always.

Nothing is auto-sent. Every reply, indent and order is drafted by Brik and held until your project manager approves it. Authority preserved. Record intact.

ii. Grounding

Answers from your project. Nothing else.

Every figure Brik gives, stock, attendance, progress, drawings, comes from your live project data, not a generic template or an invented number.

iii. Adoption

Built for the site, not the office.

One app, in the languages the crew speaks, as simple as the chat they already use. The single largest reason construction software fails is that the site won't touch it, so Brikito is designed for the people on the ground first.

iv. Privacy

Selfie is presence, not surveillance.

Attendance confirms someone is on site, it doesn't track or identify beyond that. Each person sees only what their role permits, scoped per project. Data is hosted in India and exportable on demand.
